Affiliate marketing

Welcome all the new affiliate marketers! This easy guide was made for simplifying the start of your journey in running the campaigns.

First of all, lets set it straight: what is affiliate marketing? Affiliate marketing is a sphere of online marketing where the promoter (aka affiliate) runs a campaign for the advertiser and receives payments based on the number of desired actions performed by users. Such actions may include:

- registration (single-opt-in)
- registration with confirmation (double-opt-in)
- install
- purchase and many more

In the affiliate marketing world these offers are generalized in the term CPA (cost per action). It is an affiliate marketers work to prepare the creatives for a certain CPA offer, to choose the promotion texts and messages and to create the campaign with all the needed settings. It is also their task to run and optimize the campaigns, as the result of promotion directly influences the amount of profit they get in the end. In the endpoint the affiliate gets paid for the full targeted action performed by the landing page visitors.

Pros of affiliate marketing

It may seem like a lot of work, but it is all worth it in the end. In fact, according to 2023 statistics affiliate marketing industry is worth 17$ billion dollars. Outside of being a conveniently remote job affiliate marketing has a lot of other advantages. What makes experts stay and newcomers join?

1) Easy to start
It is not difficult to start in affiliate marketing. As any other work it will take time searching for the offers and examples of successful campaigns. Probably the most time-consuming would be the actual running and optimizing the campaign for the first time. And even after that when youve found the profitable model, it would easily turn into passive income.

2) Easy to manage
Knowing the main optimization strategies and actively using them would put you onto the next level of managing the campaign. Once youve tested all the options and found out the best-performing settings it is easy to manage the campaign by running small tests for improving the already profitable result.

3) Lots of opportunities
The affiliate marketing world is huge. There are multiple verticals and niches, lots of online programs and sources for finding offers and contacting the advertisers. You can find blogs, forums, and various articles on the related subjects. And the best thing is that it keeps expanding. The previous 5 years were just the beginning of online marketing which gives this sphere endless growth opportunities.

4) Easy to profit
As mentioned before, there are a lot of offer types along with CPA conditions. Payouts can also depend on the geographical locations wanted by the advertisers (usually Tier 3 countries have the lowest payouts). Earning with CPA is not hard. In order to run a profitable campaign the job of the affiliate marketer is to create a working model for the campaign, taking in mind all the settings and requirements set by the advertiser.

There are few crucial stages of campaign preparation, so here is a detailed step-by-step guide to check yourself:

1) Understand the target audience
Choosing the offer is only a part of the job. The other part requires setting a campaign and choosing the correct settings. For that an affiliate marketer needs to identify the offers target audience. It is not efficient to set random settings without clearly seeing a vision of the user youd like to reach. This first step will set the success of your future company from the start.

PRO TIP: There is no need to write the full target audience strategy for every campaign. The affiliate marketer needs to know at least the basic indicators: geo location, language and user interests. Additionally, other indicators can be included: gender, users active hours, etc.

2) Set objectives
The main goal setting in the affiliate's planning process involves understanding the objectives of a campaign. Depending on the offer the aims may be gaining impressions, improving business image or getting leads. In the affiliate marketing world it is usually the latest. Creating and running the campaign is often not enough to reach this goal and additional improvement strategies should be applied.

3) Limit the testing budget
The prerequisite of the campaign testing is setting the right amount of budget. It helps to exclude the overspending on the certain settings and to run the campaign effectively. Adavice Teams advice is to limit your testing budget to x3 amount of your offers payout. If the offers results after optimization are not showing the improvement it is better to try a different promotion strategy or to change the offer.

4) Prepare the creatives
When having an offer on your hand you can choose the few ad formats you would like to try out with it. Based on the chosen ad formats the affiliate can start preparing the variety of creatives to test. However, the campaign's creative strategies do not solely involve colorful pictures. The best beginner-friendly ad formats are pop and push traffic campaigns.

When developing the creative materials for pop campaign think ahead of:

- funnel: test out few prelanders options, pop-ups and and engaging quizzes for users
- design of the offer: if its possible to change try to keep all creatives in one visual style, use visible CTA buttons and attractive moving effects on the landing and prelanders

When creating a push campaign plan and search for these details ahead (additionally to the list above):

- good-quality push images
- text variations to test
- set of emojis matching the offer to test
